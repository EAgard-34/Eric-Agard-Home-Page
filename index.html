Early Life

Eric Agard was born in the small island of Trinidad and Tobago. His parents Carlton and Julia Agard were hard working people that lived on the outskirts of the Capital City of Port of Spain.
Humble beginnings for Eric and his 4 other siblings, born in 4th place Eric was never the center of attention so he grew up in the shadows as it were.
Shy and not well spoken, he failed socailly but academically he had success. At an early age he was very sensitive and aware of people and his surroundings.
He was very obedient, quiet and never gave trouble to his parents or his teachers. He spent most of his time home enjoying the simple things, television and a vivid imagination.
As the years went by his ability to effectively communicate led to his frustration within the household. Frustrated and confuse he widthdrew learning to trust only himself. He found other interest, sports and music. Cricket, he played in Primary School, Secondary, University and even for his Country. Captained the Cricket team for his Primary and Secondary Schools.
